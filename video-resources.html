Video Resources

Visual learning resources about ADHD in girls and practical strategies

Understanding ADHD in Girls

Learn about how ADHD presents differently in girls, why it's often missed, and what makes the female ADHD experience unique. This video explores masking, internalized symptoms, and why girls with ADHD are frequently underdiagnosed.

ADHD: 21 Signs in Women & Girls

An in-depth look at the signs of ADHD in women and girls, highlighting the unique challenges they face. This video helps identify symptoms that are often overlooked or misattributed in females.

Symptoms of ADHD in Girls

This video helps in recognizing the symptoms of inattentive ADHD, which are more common among girls and often overlooked. Learn about the unique presentation of ADHD in girls and why it's frequently missed.

Supporting Your Daughter with ADHD

For parents: Learn about the emotional lives of girls with ADHD and how to support your daughter. This video covers understanding her unique needs, emotional regulation, and building a supportive environment.

Focus Strategies That Work

Practical techniques for improving focus and attention, including the Pomodoro Technique, body doubling, environmental design, and other evidence-based methods specifically helpful for ADHD brains.

Emotional Regulation for ADHD

Understanding emotional intensity in ADHD and learning strategies to manage big feelings, rejection sensitive dysphoria (RSD), and overwhelm. Includes grounding techniques and self-compassion practices.

Executive Functions Explained

An easy-to-understand explanation of executive functions and how ADHD affects them. Learn about working memory, task initiation, planning, and organization challenges, plus practical workarounds and fixes.

Study Tips for ADHD Teens

Study strategies designed specifically for ADHD brains. Learn about active learning techniques, breaking down assignments, using timers effectively, and creating study environments that support focus.
